Meet Our Partners

At King's Marketing & Technology Soluctions LLC we do not believe in the word employee. Every member of our team is a partner and is invested in the success of our company as well as yours.

Max King, CEO

Max is a hands on CEO with a passion for connecting businesses to their customers. Max fell in love with all aspects of advertising while attending the Kelley Schoool of Business at Indiana University and never looked back. He received his bachelors in Marekting and Advertising at the Bloomington campus. He concentrated on studying the psychology of marketing and advertising. He worked for one of his advertising professors conducting research with a focus on projecting into the mind of the consumer and studying behavioral patterns. While in school he also worked for the Universities in-house web development center, IU Communications. The web was a perfect venue to blend his passion for advertising and technology.

To expand his knowledge in the growing technology field Max decide to stay in Bloomington and received his Masters in Technology also from the Kelley School of Business.

In his free time he enjoys playing hockey, going out with friends, building websites, watching movies, trying new restaurants, and taking his goldendoodle, Arnold, to the dog beach.

Bunama Jagne, Solutions Architect

Bunama is a Software Engineer who specializes in the design, development and delivery of software solutions. He was very passionate about Technology since the early age of 10 when he would help his Dad fix Computer hardware for family and friends. He decided to pursue a degree in Computer science after high school which proved tougher than he had imagined. After graduating with a bachelor of science in Computer Science, he got an opportunity to work in the Ecommerce field which vastly improved his business experience and provided a great deal of challenges that improved his knowledge base tremendously. He is proficient in Java, .NET, JSP, HTML, JS, CSS and has worked with tehnologies such as ATG web commerce, Hibernate and MongoDB.

On a personal level, Bunama enjoys constantly learning about new technologies, playing soccer, working out and going to the beach.
